Ingredients (10 items)

500g Chicken breast, diced
400ml Coconut milk
3 tbsp Red curry paste
1 Red bell pepper, sliced
1 cup Bamboo shoots
2 Kaffir lime leaves
2 tbsp Fish sauce
1 tbsp Palm sugar
Fresh basil leaves
2 tbsp Vegetable oil

Instructions

1

Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat.

2

Add red curry paste and fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

3

Add chicken pieces and stir-fry until they turn white on the outside.

4

Pour in coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.

5

Add bell peppers, bamboo shoots, kaffir lime leaves, fish sauce, and palm sugar. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.

6

Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
