Frequently Asked Questions

How does imkitchen handle complex recipes with advance prep?

That's our superpower! When you add a recipe that needs marinating or overnight prep, imkitchen automatically schedules it on days you have time. You'll get morning reminders telling you exactly when to start prep work—so that chicken tikka masala gets marinated 4 hours before cooking time. No more avoiding recipes because of timing complexity.

Can I customize my meal plan after it's generated?

Absolutely! You can replace individual meals with alternatives from your recipe library. Just click on any meal and choose a different recipe that matches the course type.

Why appetizer, main, dessert instead of breakfast, lunch, dinner?

imkitchen focuses on **lunch** planning with a complete three-course structure. This course-based model (appetizer, main course, dessert) gives you variety within each meal and makes it easy to balance nutrition, complexity, and prep time. Think of it like planning restaurant-quality meals at home—except without the stress.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes up to 10 recipes, full weekly meal planning (7 complete three-course lunches), automatic shopping list generation, advance prep reminders, and access to thousands of community recipes. It's perfect for trying out imkitchen and expanding your cooking repertoire.

How does the shopping list work?

imkitchen automatically aggregates all ingredients from your weekly meal plan, combines duplicate items, and organizes them by category (produce, dairy, meat, pantry). You can check off items as you shop in-app.

Can I share my recipes with others?

Yes! You can share recipes to the community where other users can discover, rate, and add them to their own libraries. Community recipes help everyone discover new favorites.

Does imkitchen work offline?

Yes! imkitchen is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that caches your recipes and meal plans for offline access. Perfect for cooking in the kitchen without worrying about internet connectivity.
