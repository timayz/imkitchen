What happens when you generate a meal plan?

Meals are assigned to each day

Your recipes are distributed across the week to create a varied, balanced meal plan with a main course each day.

You can regenerate anytime

Don't like the plan? Regenerate individual days or the entire week from the Calendar page.

Kitchen Mode activates

Once a plan exists, Kitchen Mode guides you step-by-step through each day's recipes with timers and checklists.
