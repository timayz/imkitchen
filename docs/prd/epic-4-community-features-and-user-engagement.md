# Epic 4: Community Features & User Engagement

**Expanded Goal:** Enable recipe rating system and community engagement features that provide content quality validation and drive long-term user retention. This epic creates sustainable content improvement mechanisms and social engagement that transforms imkitchen from a personal tool into a community-driven platform supporting recipe discovery and sharing.

## Story 4.1: Recipe Rating and Review System
**As a** user who has cooked recipes from my collection,  
**I want** to rate and review recipes based on my experience,  
**so that** I can remember which recipes worked well and help other users discover quality content.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. 5-star rating system for recipe quality and difficulty assessment
2. Written review functionality with character limits and moderation
3. Personal rating history showing user's past recipe feedback
4. Rating aggregation displaying average scores across all users
5. Sort and filter recipes by community ratings and personal ratings
6. Rating submission only after recipe appears in user's meal plan history

## Story 4.2: Community Recipe Discovery and Sharing
**As a** user looking to expand my recipe collection,  
**I want** to discover highly-rated recipes from other users,  
**so that** I can add proven, community-validated recipes to my collection.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Community recipe browser with rating and popularity sorting
2. Recipe search across community database by ingredients or cuisine type
3. One-tap recipe import from community to personal collection
4. User-contributed recipe submissions with quality review process
5. Recipe sharing functionality allowing users to contribute their favorites
6. Community recipe metrics showing cooking frequency and success rates

## Story 4.3: User-Generated Content Moderation
**As a** platform maintaining recipe quality,  
**I want** automated and manual content moderation systems,  
**so that** community recipes maintain high standards and user safety.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Automated content filtering for inappropriate language and spam
2. Community reporting system for problematic recipes or reviews
3. Admin interface (TwinSpark/Rust backend) for content moderation
4. User reputation system based on contribution quality and community feedback
5. Recipe verification process for user-submitted content
6. Abuse prevention mechanisms including rate limiting and account restrictions

## Story 4.4: Social Engagement and Recognition Features
**As a** active community member,  
**I want** recognition for my contributions and ability to follow other users,  
**so that** I feel motivated to participate and can discover content from trusted sources.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. User profiles showing contribution statistics and favorite recipes
2. Achievement badges for recipe contributions, ratings, and community participation
3. Following system allowing users to discover recipes from trusted contributors
4. Featured recipe promotion highlighting exceptional community content
5. Contributor leaderboards showcasing most helpful community members
6. Social sharing integration for successful meal plans and recipe discoveries

## Story 4.5: Community-Driven Recipe Improvement
**As a** user who has modified a community recipe,  
**I want** to suggest improvements and see recipe variations,  
**so that** recipes evolve and improve based on collective cooking experience.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Recipe variation submission system for community recipe improvements
2. Ingredient substitution suggestions based on community modifications
3. Cooking tip and technique sharing attached to specific recipes
4. Recipe version history showing evolution based on community feedback
5. Collaborative recipe editing for original contributors
6. Community voting on suggested recipe improvements and modifications
