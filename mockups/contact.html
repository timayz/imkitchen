Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium from your profile settings or dashboard. Choose between monthly ($9.99/mo) or annual ($59.94/yr) plans.

Can I import my existing recipes?

Yes! Use our bulk import feature with JSON files. Download our schema documentation for the correct format.

How does meal plan generation work?

Our algorithm generates meal plans for all remaining weeks of the current month, considering your dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, and available recipes.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your profile settings. You'll retain Premium access until the end of your billing period.
