Recipe Details

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 400ml Coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp Red curry paste
  • 1 cup Jasmine rice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add curry paste and fry for 1 minute
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Serve over jasmine rice
