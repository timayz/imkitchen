# Project Brief: imkitchen

## Executive Summary

**imkitchen** is a mobile application that eliminates the mental overhead of meal planning through intelligent automation, enabling users to access their full recipe collection without timing complexity barriers. The app solves the core problem where home cooks artificially limit their culinary variety to avoid advance preparation coordination challenges.

Targeting home cooking enthusiasts who currently self-restrict their recipe choices due to planning complexity, imkitchen delivers automated weekly meal planning with intelligent scheduling that considers availability, energy levels, and ingredient management. The key value proposition is transforming meal planning from a cognitively demanding chore into an effortless "Fill My Week" experience, unlocking culinary creativity previously constrained by timing logistics.

## Problem Statement

**Current State & Pain Points:**
Home cooking enthusiasts maintain collections of favorite recipes but systematically avoid complex dishes that require advance preparation timing. The current process involves a multi-phase workflow: weekly selection → ingredient planning → advance preparation → daily execution. Users rely on manual systems (browser favorites, mental tracking) that create cognitive overhead and decision paralysis.

**Quantified Impact:**

- Users artificially constrain their recipe repertoire to avoid timing coordination complexity
- Advanced preparation requirements create "artificial barriers to recipe exploration"
- Manual planning systems generate significant cognitive load that "limits culinary creativity"
- Self-limiting behavior reduces culinary variety, with users missing access to their full recipe collection
- Daily cooking decisions become stressful rather than enjoyable due to inadequate advance planning

**Why Existing Solutions Fall Short:**
Current meal planning apps focus on recipe discovery or basic calendar organization, but fail to address the core automation gap. They don't solve the fundamental timing orchestration problem that causes users to self-limit their choices. Generic meal planning tools require users to manually coordinate advance preparation, shopping logistics, and daily execution timing - perpetuating rather than eliminating the cognitive burden.

**Urgency & Importance:**
The cooking behavior pattern of self-limitation represents massive latent value - users want to access their full recipe collection but lack tools that make complex cooking achievable within their daily routines. As home cooking increases post-pandemic, the demand for intelligent kitchen automation has never been higher. Users are ready to trust automated systems if they demonstrably optimize their time and reduce mental overhead.

## Proposed Solution

**Core Concept & Approach:**
imkitchen delivers intelligent meal planning automation through a "Fill My Week" system that orchestrates the complete cooking workflow from weekly selection through daily execution. The solution centers on an Advanced Intelligent Scheduling Engine that considers multiple factors: user availability, energy levels, ingredient freshness, and advance preparation timing to automatically generate optimized meal plans.

**Key Differentiators:**

- **Multi-Factor Optimization**: Unlike basic meal planners, imkitchen's scheduling algorithm weighs availability, energy states, ingredient lifecycle, and equipment conflicts to create truly intelligent meal timing
- **No-Duplicate Rotation System**: Ensures users experience their full recipe collection through systematic rotation until all favorites are cooked
- **Detailed Morning Prep Reminders**: Provides specific timing notifications with task duration estimates, eliminating the guesswork that causes users to avoid complex recipes
- **One-Tap Easy Mode**: Adaptive system that recognizes low-energy days and automatically suggests simpler alternatives
- **Community-Driven Content**: User-generated recipes and social meal plan sharing create sustainable content growth and engagement

**Why This Solution Will Succeed:**
The solution directly addresses the core user insight: "I don't want to miss any recipes" by removing timing complexity as a barrier. Users will trust the system because it optimizes their day-time rather than creating additional overhead. The combination of intelligent automation with community features creates both immediate utility and long-term engagement, while the freemium model with grocery affiliate partnerships ensures sustainable monetization.

**High-Level Product Vision:**
A comprehensive cooking ecosystem where users simply indicate their recipe preferences and availability, then receive fully orchestrated meal plans with automated shopping lists, precise timing notifications, and emergency alternatives for life disruptions. The platform evolves into a community hub where successful meal plans are shared, recipes are collaboratively improved, and grocery ordering becomes seamless.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features (Must Have)

- **"Fill My Week" Button with Rotation Logic**: One-tap weekly meal generation that cycles through user's recipe collection with no-duplicate constraint until all favorites are cooked. Essential for eliminating planning decision fatigue while ensuring recipe variety.

- **Visual Meal Calendar Interface**: Week-view displaying breakfast/lunch/dinner slots with prep requirement indicators, color coding for complexity levels, and advance preparation flags. Provides essential UX foundation for all meal planning interactions.

- **Basic Recipe Management System**: User favorites import/entry, recipe database with prep time indicators, and simple categorization. Core data foundation enabling personalized meal rotation and planning.

- **Simple Shopping List Generation**: Automated ingredient consolidation from weekly meal selections with basic grocery store organization (produce, dairy, pantry). Delivers immediate utility demonstrating app value.

- **Community Recipe Ratings**: User feedback system for recipe quality and difficulty validation. Builds engagement from day one and provides content quality signals for future intelligent features.

### Out of Scope for MVP

- User-generated recipe creation tools
- Social meal plan sharing features
- Emergency meal alternatives system
- Detailed morning prep notifications with timing
